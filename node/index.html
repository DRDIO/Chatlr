Tumblr Chat

Help Main Room

Commands — Tumblr Chat supports the following commands:

  • /help
  • /topic
  • /away
  • /ignore USERNAME
  • /room ROOMNAME
  • /rooms
  • /mobile

Rules — No bullying or griefing is allowed. Self promoters and annoying people will be kicked to the Spam Room. Repeated vulgarity or attacks will result in a ban. If you are in any of the primary language rooms, please remain civil towards others. All chats are logged, even private ones. Have fun and be nice to others.

About — Tumblr Chat was created by Kevin Nuut using Tumblr OAuth, Socket.IO, Node.JS, jQuery, and PHP. The views expressed in this chat do not necessarily represent the views of Tumblr. We use your credentials to read your blog titles, names, urls, and avatars. We do not write anything to your blog.

Problems — Please report all problems to Kevin Nuut's Ask Box.
